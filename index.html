Performance Fabrics

Every garment features two or more advanced fabrics. Choose the tech level that matches your riding conditions.

Lightweight Tech Fabrics

FabricBest For
Tech 1.0Warm weather. Maximum breathability and moisture wicking.
Tech 2.0All-around performance. Balanced breathability and stretch.
Tech 3.0Premium hand feel. Enhanced moisture management.
Tech 4.1Ultra-lightweight. Ideal for hot conditions and high intensity.

FabricBest For

LW Thermal FleeceCool weather. Light insulation with moisture wicking.
MW Thermal FleeceCold weather. Heavier insulation for winter rides.

How to Order

Every order is custom-made to your specifications.

1

Browse & Configure

Explore designs. Select your preferred fabric, fit, and size for each item.

2

Add to Cart

Build your full kit. Add as many items as you'd like to your cart.

3

Request a Quote

Submit your cart with your contact info. CustomWear will send you a personalized quote.

4

Receive Your Kit

Once confirmed, your custom kit is produced and shipped directly to you.
